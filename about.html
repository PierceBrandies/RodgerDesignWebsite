About Us

Who we are

We are a family run business with over 30 years of experience designing and building clients dream kitchens.

Located at the foot of the blue mountains, in the rapidly expanding suburb of Penrith, we have been servicing homes all over the greater sydney region and beyond.

From coast to country we have delivered bespoke cabinetry and furniture to our loyal clients.

Our services

Our background is in furniture craftsmanship, so no job is too unique.

Although we may be best known for our stunning kitchen renovations, our clients will tell you that we have gone on to provide a wide variety of custom built solutions to meet their tailored needs in every space.

From butlers pantries to outdoor barbeques.

From luxe bars to bedside tables.

Our passion is in craftmanship in every part of the home.

Our process

Our process starts with you.

Once you have reached out for a free consultation, we will work with you to design a solution for your space.

Unlike comercialised competitors, we are a small family team that designs, builds and installs in house from start to finish.

This means that as one of our clients you can rest assured that the process will be collaborative and transparent so that your vision can be executed with perfection.
